Why a New Institution?

Existing international financial institutions and climate funds have inadequately addressed the urgent adaptation needs of the most vulnerable, particularly Least Developed Countries and Small Island Developing States facing extreme weather events, sea-level rise, and ecosystem degradation.

IBCA is designed to fill this critical gap with a narrow, legally binding mandate that prevents mission creep.
