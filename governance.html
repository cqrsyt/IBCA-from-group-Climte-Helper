Governance Structure

Assembly of Parties, Standing Committee, President’s Office and Secretariat

Organisational Structure

Assembly of Parties (Plenary Organ)

  • Approve Annual Budgets
  • Decide Fund Allocation
  • Admission / Removal of members (2/3 majority)
  • Normal decisions: Double-majority (60% total votes + 60% recipient-country votes)

Standing Committee (Executive Organ)

  • Make operational decisions between annual sessions
  • Recommend the President
  • Supervise the Secretariat
  • Major financial decisions e.g. loans (2/3 majority)

President’s Office

  • Principal Executive Official
  • Public face of the Bank
  • Discuss and make final executive decisions

Secretariat

Handles the day-to-day work of the Bank under the supervision of the Standing Committee.

Decision-making Mechanism

Body Matter Threshold
Assembly of Parties Admission / Removal 2/3 majority
Assembly of Parties Normal cases Double-majority (60% total + 60% recipient)
Assembly / Standing Committee Emergency Sessions / Actions Unanimity
Standing Committee Recommendation to Appoint President Unanimity
Standing Committee Major Financial Decisions (e.g. loans) 2/3 majority
